You enter the first door towards your left and
get immediately hit with the smell of roses and lavender.

You look in the direction of the scent and your brain resets your vision,
making you see the vase on the table.

The smell being replaced with a dusty manor,
the type that hasn't been managed in a very long time.

Second thoughts plague your mind as the dust settles on your armour. You start to head out to the front.

You see only a door surrounded in thorny vines and overgrown thickets of grass.
