The Prince Who Became an Officeworker

Once upon a time there was a prince who had dreams.

Dreams to contribute to something bigger than himself (a giant company). Dreams to create (word documents). Dreams to live...

...as an officeworker...

...

Did I hear that correctly? Seriously? Nevermind...

Unfortunately (for obvious reasons) his parents (the literal king and queen) did not aprove.

"Don't be ridiculous!" they scoffed (and rightfully so). They did not support his dreams.

So the prince did the only thing he could think of...

He renounced his title and moved to the big city to become an officeworker.

And so he lived happily ever after...
