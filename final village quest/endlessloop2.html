You enter the door right in the middle, expecting some
mysterious or great monster to appear for additional experience.

Your thoughts were shaken up by seeing similar objects placed around the area.

You had realized that you were walking in the same corridor
as you see the same vase and table for the fifth time in a row.

Although you have not learnt how to get out of this predicament,
you decided to leave in search of a new lead.

You step out of the entrance and a bright light burns your eyes,
but you still feel like you have seen this before.
