The Prince Becomes a Hero

"I will put this plainly, we need your help, Salix."

"H...how do you know my name?"

"That is not the point. The evil king and his monsters took over our kingdom. You have potential, you can help."

"But I am just a plain officeworker, how could I possibly help?"

"A prophecy states that only a fallen prince may save out kingdom. The summoning spell I performed led me to you... But if you are just an officeworker perhaps I have made an error..."

"No...You are correct...I am a fallen prince."

"I knew there was hope! I must bring you to my world quickly, we don't have time to spare!""

And so you begin to feel lightheaded and close your eyes...
