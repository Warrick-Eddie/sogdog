The Mundane Life of an Officeworker

Unfortunately, the life of an officeworker, though simple, was unfulfilling.

Everyday was filled with the same, repetitive, boring tasks.

He had to do his own chores, commute on public transport, and worst of all...

He could not eat the luxurious food he once enjoyed as a prince.

(He also had no friends but that is beside the point I guess.)

He had become sick of this lifestyle.

As always Salix was working overtime. The day was average and boring, just like it was everyday.

He finds himself become dreary and begins to fall asleep...
